How to Use

  1. Upload an image file (JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP, or SVG)
  2. Select the favicon sizes you want to generate
  3. Click "Generate Favicons" to create your favicons
  4. Download individual sizes or download all at once
  5. Use the generated .ico files in your website

Tips

  • For best results, use a square image (1:1 aspect ratio)
  • Recommended minimum size: 512x512 pixels
  • Simple designs work best at small sizes
  • 16x16 and 32x32 are the most commonly used sizes
